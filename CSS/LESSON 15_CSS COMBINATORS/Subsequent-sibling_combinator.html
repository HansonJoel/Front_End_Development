Subsequent-Sibling Combinator (~)

Also known as General Sibling Selector, Selects all elements that are next siblings of a specified element.
Syntax: selector1 ~ selector2
h2 ~ p: This selects all <p> elements that are siblings of and come after any <h2> element.
